Getting started

What equipment does Smarter Pool work with?
Smarter Pool is designed for Pentair EasyTouch pool control systems. It communicates with your EasyTouch gateway over your home Wi-Fi network using the ScreenLogic protocol. Other Pentair systems may work but are not officially supported yet.
Do I need any special hardware?
Yes — the Smarter Pool kit includes a pre-configured controller (Orange Pi Zero 3) and a YINMIK YK-S03 7-in-1 pool sensor. The kit connects to your existing Pentair EasyTouch gateway. No rewiring or electrician is required.
How long does setup take?
Most users are up and running in 30–60 minutes. You'll connect the controller to your home Wi-Fi, place the sensor in your pool, and download the app. A step-by-step setup guide is included in the kit and in the app.
Does it work without an internet connection?
Yes — the Free plan works entirely on your local home network, no internet required. Pro and Family plan features like remote access, push notifications, and AI assistant require an internet connection.

Sensor & chemistry

Why is my pH reading showing an unexpected value?
If your pH shows a value like 79 instead of 7.9, make sure you have the latest version of the app installed. This was a known display issue in earlier versions that has been corrected. The sensor itself reports accurate values.
How often does the sensor update?
Sensor readings refresh every 30 seconds in the app. The YINMIK YK-S03 sensor transmits data continuously via the Tuya Cloud API.
Where should I place the sensor in my pool?
Place the sensor in a shaded area of the pool, away from return jets and direct sunlight. The middle of the pool at a depth of 12–18 inches gives the most representative readings.

App & account

The app shows "Connecting…" and won't connect to my pool.
This usually means the app can't reach the Smarter Pool controller on your network. Try: (1) Make sure your phone and the controller are on the same Wi-Fi network. (2) Restart the controller by unplugging it for 10 seconds. (3) Check that your Pentair EasyTouch gateway is powered on. If the issue persists, email support@lewistech.io.
How do I cancel my subscription?
Subscriptions are managed through Apple. Go to Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions on your iPhone to cancel. Your free plan access continues after cancellation.
Is Pool Girl Deb always available?
Pool Girl Deb requires an active Pro or Family subscription and an internet connection. She's available 24/7 and responds in seconds. Her responses are powered by Anthropic's Claude AI.
