Privacy Policy

Lewis Tech LLC — Smarter Pool Privacy Policy
Last updated: June 2026

What we collect
Smarter Pool collects pool chemistry readings (pH, chlorine, ORP, temperature) and equipment status from your pool controller. This data is stored locally on your device and optionally synced to our secure cloud (Supabase) for historical trends and alerts.

What we don't collect
We do not collect your name, address, payment information, or any personally identifiable information without your explicit consent. We do not sell your data to third parties.

AI assistant
When you use Pool Girl Deb, your messages are sent to Anthropic's API (claude.ai) via our secure proxy. Anthropic's privacy policy applies to these interactions. We do not store your AI conversation history.

Local network
The app communicates directly with your Smarter Pool controller on your local WiFi network. No pool control commands are routed through external servers.
